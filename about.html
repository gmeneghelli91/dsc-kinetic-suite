About Me

Hello! I'm Giovanni Meneghelli, a dedicated R&D professional with over a decade of hands-on experience in the chemical and pharmaceutical industries. My career has been a journey of growth, spanning from foundational work in medicinal chemistry to my current specialization in industrial process development and material science.

Currently, I am an R&D Lab Technician at F.I.S. - Fabbrica Italiana Sintetici. Here, my work is centered in the Calorimetry and Particle Technology department, where I focus on the deep characterization of materials to ensure process safety, stability, and efficiency.

This website is a passion project born directly from my daily work. I developed these tools to streamline the analysis of complex data and to share practical solutions with a community of fellow chemists, researchers, and students.
